What to Look For:

  • Top-Left to Bottom-Right (\): Uses rotate(45deg) - stripe goes from top-left to bottom-right
  • Bottom-Left to Top-Right (/): Uses rotate(-45deg) - stripe goes from bottom-left to top-right
  • Fixed version (80px): Thin diagonal stripe, text readable at any rotation
  • Old version (10px): Only shows a tiny red triangle in bottom-left corner

Technical Details:

The fix uses height: 80px for a thin stripe, positioned at center with transform: translate(-50%, -50%) rotate(±45deg) and width: 200vmax to ensure it spans the full diagonal from corner to corner.

Position Options:

  • position: 'diagonal' or 'diagonal-tlbr' - Top-left to bottom-right (\)
  • position: 'diagonal-bltr' - Bottom-left to top-right (/)
